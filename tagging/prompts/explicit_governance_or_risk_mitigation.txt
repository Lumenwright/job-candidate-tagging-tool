Detect whether the candidate describes a CONCRETE risk, governance, ethics,
security, or privacy mechanism they treated as a design constraint.

FIRES WHEN the text names a specific mechanism such as:
- human-in-the-loop review, approval gates, or a human veto on AI output, or
- evaluation guardrails (hallucination/quality monitoring, automated checks), or
- data privacy or PII handling (redaction, access restriction, retention limits), or
- security boundaries (sandboxing, permissioning, isolation), or
- compliance / regulatory / audit measures (audit logging, policy enforcement).

DOES NOT FIRE WHEN the text only:
- uses buzzwords ("responsible AI", "ethical", "trustworthy") with no mechanism, or
- claims to "care about quality/safety" without saying what was done, or
- refers to project/schedule/business risk rather than AI or data risk, or
- mentions governance only as a topic without the candidate having acted on it.

Cite the exact span describing the mechanism. The mechanism must be something the
candidate did or designed, not a general value statement.
